About Chakri

Connecting talented professionals with their dream opportunities since 2023

Our Mission

At Chakri, we're on a mission to transform how people find jobs and how companies find talent. We believe that the right job can change a person's life, and the right talent can transform a business.

We strive to create a platform that makes the job search process more efficient, transparent, and fair for everyone involved.

Our Journey

2023

The Beginning

Chakri was founded with a simple idea: make job searching better for everyone. We started with a small team and big dreams.

2023

First 10,000 Users

Within six months, we reached our first milestone of 10,000 registered users and 1,000 companies.

2024

National Expansion

We expanded our services nationwide, reaching all major cities and establishing partnerships with top companies.

2025

Going Global

Today, we're taking Chakri global, with our platform available in multiple languages and serving job seekers worldwide.
